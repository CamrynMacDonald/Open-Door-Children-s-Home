Design Challenge

I chose the organization Open Door Children's Home, an organization located in my hometown of Rome, Georgia. The Home focuses on children whose parents are unable to take care of them, who have experienced trauma or abuse, and even siblings in the foster care system who do not wish to be split up.

What is the problen you're trying to solve?

To increase financial and item donations for the Open Door Children's Home.

Framed as a Design Question

How can the Open Door Home's website increase donations for kids in need?

Ultimate Impact

To increase the number of donations to improve the lives of the children of the Open Door Home.

What are some possible solutions to your problem?

Clear donation options, Visible call to action, Readable and condensed copy, Hierarchy, Clear contacts and location.

Content and Constraints

Limited to only Rome, Georiga area, and a Small organization.

Design Question Restated

How can the website design reach a large variety of people and promote both fiancial and item donations?
