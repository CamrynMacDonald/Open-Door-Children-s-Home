Primary Client Research

The Open Door Children's Home main goal is to provide needs for dependent, abused, and/or neglected children in the Northwest Georgia area to help give them and inspire them to live better lives. The Home offers different options to volunteer, give, and impact to improve the children of the Home's lives. The Open Door Home's goals can be achieved by offering volunteer services and options to donate items and meet financial needs.

The website explains the many different services the Open Door Home provides, including offering basic needs like food and clothing, a nurturing environment, health services, and an emphasis on academics.

The Open Door Home also hosts many fundraisers and events, such as golf tournaments and lemonade stands, to raise awareness and financial donations for basic needs and improvements for the Home.

Recent News

In August 2017, the Open Door Home was featured in the local newspaper, Rome News Tribune, for welcoming in children with behavioral needs. The Home's 'administration' revealed that while these children had more needs, they are glad that they have a home at Open Door. The article also revealed the Home's need for more funds to use toward the Home and the children. A local lemonade and cookies stand run by a young boy raises money for the Home 'every year', but more donations were needed.

Social Media

The Open Door Home has a prevalent following on Facebook, and features recent posts about any events or updates about the Home. The Facebook page features photos of donors, recent fundraisers, and any recent needs of the Home.
