Determine Audience and Goals

After conducting research, we focused on finding the website's specific audience and cooresponding goals.

Audience

This part of the research was about determining our target audiences. We began with a broad approach and then narrowed down to the most effected audience, all while using lots of sticky notes.

We first looked at the main audience and think of all the people who might possibly visit the website. I focused on those who need help, those who could help, and those who might be opposed or competitors of the organization.

I then focused more on who could help, and placed them in different subcategories based on how they might effect the organization. I divided the notes up into those who are involved or are a part of the Open Door Children's Home, those that would offer donations, and those that would volunteer time or services.

I finally narrowed down the audience to those who know or are effected by a child who is experiencing a number of issues that could cause them to need a more stable home environment.

Define Goals

This exercise was to determine the client, audience, and website goals for the organizations we chose.

Overarching Goals

Client
  • Raise awareness of the Open Door Children's Home
  • Gain money and items from donations
  • Provide children in need a welcoming environment to live in
  • Connect with the local community
Audience
  • Learn more about the ODCH and what it does
  • Give money and/or donate items
  • Volunteer or mentor children of the ODCH
Website
  • Display information in a readable and interesting way
  • Have a visible place for donations of items or money
  • Make location and contacts easy to find
  • Display a welcoming environment for kids in need and the local community

Specific Goals

User Goal Content/Action
Parents/Teachers Spread Awareness, Receive Donations, Volunteer Mentor/volunteer with kids, Donate items and/or money, organize drives/fundraisers, Become Home Parent, Participate in Christmas drive
Churches Involve Community, Volunteers, Donations Make and serve dinners, Volunteer/mentor kids, Donate items/money, Participate in Christmas drive
Students Spread Awareness, Receive Donations, Volunteer, Learn More Find out more about ODCH, Mentor/volunteer with kids, Donate money and/or items, Participate in Christmas drive
Local Community Organizations Involve Community, Receive Donations, Volunteer, Learn More, Spread Awareness Learn more about ODCH and community, Donate items/money, Donate business services, Volunteer/mentor kids
DFAX/Foster Home Workers Learn More, Spread Awareness Learn more about the ODCH and community, Offer services to house a child at the home

Goals Conclusions

After these exercises, I finally narrowed down the main goals of the ODCH is to spread awareness about the organization while also increasing the number of financial and item donations. The audience's goals of the website are to learn more about the organization's services as well as volunteer, mentor, or donate. The website has the task of presenting these opportunites, as well as display any information about the ODCH, any fundraisers, or recent needs of the children.

These main goals and content are based on my interview with Amy Cox and the content that is currently shown on the ODCH website. The main task will be discovering how to display information about the organization so that it is clear as well as making volunteer and donation opportunities more visible.
